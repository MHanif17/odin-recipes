  • Bouncy Beef Meatball Recipe (Resep Bakso Daging Sapi Kenyal)

    The key to bouncy meatballs without preservatives is using very cold meat (you can use ice cubes) and a quick mixing process using a food processor or blender.

    Key Ingredients:

    • 500 gr ground beef, lean
    • 100 gr tapioca flour or sago flour
    • 80 - 100 gr ice cubes, crushed small
    • 1 egg white
    • 1 tsp baking powder
    • 1.5 tsp salt (or to taste)
    • 1 tsp granulated sugar
    • 1/2 tsp ground white pepper

    Fine Spices (blended/pounded):

    • 4-5 cloves garlic, fried until fragrant
    • 3 shallots, fried until fragrant

    How to Make the Meatball Dough:

    1. Process the Meat: Put the cold ground meat into a food processor or blender. Process until the meat starts to smooth out.
    2. Add Ice Cubes: While continuing to process, gradually add the crushed ice cubes. Process until the mixture becomes a smooth and thick paste.
    3. Add Spices and Other Ingredients: Add the egg white, fine spices, tapioca flour, salt, sugar, ground pepper, and baking powder. Process again until all ingredients are well mixed and the dough feels sticky and dense.
    4. Test the Taste: Take a small amount of dough, boil it or heat it in the microwave to taste test. Add more seasoning if needed.
    5. Shape the Meatballs: Boil water in a large pot, then reduce the heat so the water is just simmering slowly. Take the meatball dough in your hand, make a fist, and squeeze so the dough comes out through the gap between your thumb and index finger. Use a spoon to scoop the meatballs and gently drop them into the hot water.
    6. Cook the Meatballs: Cook the meatballs over low to medium heat. The meatballs will float to the surface when they are done. Let them float for 3-4 minutes after floating to ensure they are cooked through.
    7. Soak in Ice Water: Lift the cooked meatballs out and immediately place them in a basin of ice water to stop the cooking process and achieve a bouncy texture.
