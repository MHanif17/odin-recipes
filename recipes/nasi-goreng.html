Nasi Goreng || Indonesian Fried Rice

  • Simple Homemade Fried Rice Recipe (Resep Nasi Goreng Rumahan Sederhana)

    This fried rice recipe is easy to make with common kitchen ingredients. The key to delicious fried rice is using cold leftover rice from the night before, which is drier (pera) and not mushy.

  • Ingredients:

    • 2 plates white rice (leftover rice recommended)
    • 1 chicken egg
    • 200 gr chicken meat/sausage/meatballs, diced or sliced
    • 1 tbsp sweet soy sauce (or to taste)
    • 1 tbsp chili sauce (optional, if you want it spicy)
    • Salt, sugar, and ground pepper to taste
    • Enough cooking oil for sautéing

  • Fine Spices (or finely minced):

    • 4 cloves shallots
    • 3 cloves garlic
    • 2 red curly chilies (or to taste)
    • 1 bird's eye chili (optional, if you like it very spicy)

  • Accompaniments (optional):

    • Crackers (kerupuk)
    • Cucumber and carrot pickles (acar)
    • Fried egg (telur mata sapi)
    • Slices of fresh cucumber

  • Steps for Making Fried Rice:

    1. Prepare the spices: Grind the shallots, garlic, and chilies using a blender or mortar and pestle.
    2. Sauté the spices: Heat a little cooking oil in a wok/pan. Add the fine spices, sauté until fragrant and cooked through.
    3. Cook the fillings: Add the diced chicken, sausage, or meatballs. Cook until they change color and are cooked through. Push them to the side of the pan.
    4. Cook the egg: Add the chicken egg, scramble it until cooked. Mix with the other fillings.
    5. Add the rice: Add the white rice. Use medium-high heat. Stir quickly until all the spices and ingredients are evenly mixed with the rice.
    6. Season: Add the sweet soy sauce, chili sauce (if using), salt, sugar, and ground pepper. Continue stirring quickly until the fried rice is evenly coated with the seasoning and has a nice color.
    7. Taste test: Taste a little, add seasoning if anything is missing. Cook for a little longer until the rice is thoroughly hot.
    8. Serve: Lift the fried rice and serve it on a plate. Add accompaniments such as crackers, pickles, or a fried egg on top.
