Mie Ayam || Indonesian Chicken Noodle

    Ingredients & Steps:

  • Ingredients for Making Noodles:

    • 200 grams high-protein flour
    • 20 grams low-protein flour
    • 2 chicken eggs
    • 1 tsp salt
    • 4 tbsp vegetable oil

  • Ingredients for Sweet Soy Chicken Topping:

    • Chicken breast fillet, cut into small cubes
    • Enough clean water
    • 6 chicken feet that have been boiled/pre-cooked
    • Spring onion, finely sliced
    • 2 bay leaves (daun salam)
    • 1 lemongrass stalk, bruised
    • Sweet soy sauce (kecap manis)
    • Soy sauce (kecap asin)
    • Salt and pepper to taste
    • Cooking oil
    • Sasa MSG powder to taste (for seasoning mix when assembling the noodles)

  • Fine Spices Ingredients for Chicken Topping:

    • 7 cloves shallots, finely minced
    • 4 cloves garlic, finely minced
    • 2 cm ginger
    • 2 cm turmeric
    • ½ tsp coriander powder

  • Ingredients for Chicken Noodle Oil:

    • 150 grams chicken skin
    • 100 ml cooking oil
    • 6 cloves garlic, minced

  • Accompaniments:

    • Enough spring onions
    • Enough fried wonton crackers (kerupuk pangsit)
    • Enough chili sauce (sambal)
    • Enough blanched mustard greens (sawi)

  • Steps for Making Homemade Noodles:

    1. Mix all the eggs with salt and vegetable oil until well combined.
    2. Pour the flour into a bowl, then add the egg mixture into it, knead the dough.
    3. Make sure the dough is not too dry or wet, let it rest for 15 minutes.
    4. Roll out the dough until thin enough, then cut the dough using a noodle machine/cutter.
    5. You can process the noodles immediately or store them in a dry, airtight container.

  • Recipe for Chicken Soy Sauce Topping:

    1. Pour cooking oil into a clean pan and heat it up.
    2. Sauté the fine spices, sliced spring onion (white part), bay leaves, and lemongrass until fragrant.
    3. Add the chicken and water into it, boil until the water boils.
    4. Add the sweet soy sauce, soy sauce, salt, and pepper to taste. Stir evenly.
    5. Add the pre-boiled chicken feet. Stir again.
    6. Cook and wait until the seasoning is absorbed. If the sauce looks thick, the chicken and chicken feet are ready to be lifted and used.

  • How to Make Chicken Noodle Oil:

    1. Heat the oil, fry the chicken skin over low heat until crispy. Lift the chicken skin.
    2. Using medium heat, add the minced garlic and cook until fragrant. When it turns yellowish, lift it and it's ready to use.

  • Steps for Serving Chicken Noodles:

    1. Pour 2 tbsp of the chicken oil, 1 tbsp of soy sauce into a bowl, and Sasa MSG powder to taste.
    2. Boil enough of the noodles you made, then drain and set them aside in the bowl.
    3. Stir until the oil is evenly distributed throughout the noodles.
    4. Place the chicken topping on top of the noodles.
    5. Add enough pieces of mustard greens and spring onions.
    6. Add sweet soy sauce, fried wonton crackers, and chili sauce to taste. Your homemade chicken noodles are ready to be served!
